Explorations in ML/AI

I too have been fascinated by LLMs, and their amazing abilities. It seems very likely at this point that LLMs, and more broadly, ML/AI, will play a significant role in the future, and it is important to have a min-viable understanding of them.

Hence, over the past 6 months, I have been learning ML/AI in my spare time. I am trying to learn enough to have an intuitive mathematical understanding of these systems, without trying to be a ML researcher. I am also trying to sample from different ML fields rather than limiting myself to NLP and LLMs.
